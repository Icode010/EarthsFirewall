About Our Mission

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025

Our Team

We are SPALRB, a dedicated team participating in the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge. Our mission is to create an innovative and educational platform that simulates asteroid impact scenarios and demonstrates various planetary defense strategies.

Our Goal

To develop a realistic, interactive simulation that helps users understand the science behind asteroid impacts and the various mitigation strategies being researched by NASA and the international space community.

NASA Partnership

This project is developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge, an international hackathon that brings together coders, scientists, designers, and technologists to solve real-world problems on Earth and in space.
