Quick Start Guide

While we're developing the full tutorial system, here's a quick guide to get you started with the basic features of Earth's Firewall.

1

Launch the Simulation

Click the "Launch Simulation" button to enter the 3D environment. Take a moment to explore the interface and familiarize yourself with the controls.

2

Select an Asteroid

Choose from pre-configured asteroid scenarios or create a custom asteroid with specific parameters like size, velocity, and composition.

3

Choose Defense Strategy

Select a defense method based on the asteroid's characteristics. Consider factors like size, composition, and available lead time.

4

Run the Simulation

Execute your defense mission and observe the results. Analyze the effectiveness of your chosen strategy and learn from the outcomes.
